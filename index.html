Manos Advising

About Us

Manos advising provides data advisory services including data collecting, cleaning, analysis, and efficiency improvements to businesses and non-profit organizations alike. Our past projects include creating an inventory system with AI that automatically suggests products to order when the inventory runs low, and analysis for BoyScout and community churches to increase fundraising profits.
